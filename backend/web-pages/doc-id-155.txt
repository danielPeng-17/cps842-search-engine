LICENSE MAKER [LICENSEMAKER.JPG]

Pretend you're in prison and make a license!

California 1915 1939 1945 1956 1963 1969 1984 1987 1993 1998 AIDS UCLA

Accepts letters, digits, ".+-/:;*#!", space, and a few special characters [special.html]. Some plates allow any number of characters; others allow
seven characters max, or eight with at least one space. Graphics adapted from R.T. Brandon's Blank Plates page [http://www.blankplates.com/] and the
CA DMV's search page [http://plates.ca.gov/search/]. The character colors for some plates may not be right, feel free to send corrections. There are
currently a total of 505 different plate blanks available.
